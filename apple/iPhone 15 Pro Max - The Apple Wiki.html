The iPhone 15 Pro Max was introduced by Apple CEO Tim Cook at Apple's 'Wonderlust' event on 12 September 2023 (2023-09-12).

It was made available for preorder on 15 September 2023 (2023-09-15) and for purchase on 22 September 2023 (2023-09-22).

It uses the board configuration D84AP and the firmware identifier is iPhone16,2.

Application Processor

This model of iPhone uses Apple's A17 Pro SoC

Specifications

Display:

  • Size: 6.7 inches (diagonal).
  • Brightness: 1000 nits max brightness (typical); 1600 nits max brightness (HDR); 2000 nits max brightness (HDR).
  • Colorspace: DCI-P3
  • Technology: OLED
  • Resolution: 2796x1290 at 460 pixels per inch
  • Refresh Rate: 120Hz
  • Contrast Ratio: 2,000,000:1

Other Display Features

  • Dynamic Island
  • HDR Display
  • True Tone
  • Fingerprint-resistant oleophobic coating
  • Support for display of multiple languages and characters simultaneously.

Color Options:

  • Black Titanium
  • White Titanium
  • Blue Titanium
  • Natural Titanium

Storage Options:

  • 256GB
  • 512GB
  • 1TB

Advertised Battery Life:

  • Audio playback: Up to 95 hours
  • Video playback: Up to 29 hours
  • Video playback (streamed): Up to 25 hours

Actual Battery Specifications:

  • 4,422mAh
  • 17.109Wh
  • ?V
  • Fast-charge capable.

Voice Assistant:

  • Siri

Cameras

  • Front: 12MP camera with an /1.9 aperture.
  • Back: Triple camera system: Wide, Ultra Wide, and Telephoto.
    • 48MP, /1.78 aperture (Wide).
      • Virtual Telephoto enabled by quad-pixel sensor; 12MP, 2x, /1.78 aperture.
    • 12MP, /2.2 aperture and 120 field of view. (Ultra Wide).
    • 12MP, 2x, /1.78 aperture (Telephoto).
    • 12MP, 5x, /2.8 aperture (Telephoto).
    • 3x optical zoom in; 2x optical zoom out; 10x optical zoom range; digital zoom up to 25x

Sensors:

  • Face ID
  • LiDAR Scanner
  • Barometer
  • High dynamic range gyro
  • High-g accelerometer
  • Proximity sensor
  • Dual ambient light sensors

Size and Weight

  • Height: 6.29 inches (159.9 mm)
  • Width: 3.02 inches (76.7 mm)
  • Depth: 0.32 inch (8.25 mm)
  • Weight: 7.81 ounces (221 grams)

Cellular and Wireless

  • WiFi 6E (802.11ax) with 2x2 MIMO
  • Bluetooth 5.3 technology
  • 5G (sub6 GHz and mmWave) with 4x4 MIMO
  • Gigabit LTE with 4x4 MIMO and LAA
  • Second-generation Ultra Wideband chip
  • Thread networking technology
  • NFC with reader mode
  • Express Cards with power reserve

Connectors:

  • USB-C (USB 3 up to 10 Gb/s)
  • MagSafe
